Ingredients:
 - one 15-ounce can black beans, drained and rinsed
 - Two tomatoes finely chopped
 - Half an onion finely chopped
 - 1 cup frozen corn, thawed
 - 1 jalapeno finely chopped
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 cup cilantro finely chopped
 - 1 lemon, juiced

Directions:
 1. Take a large bowl, add the beans
 2. Add chopped tomatoes, onion, jalapeno, thawed corn and cilantro
 3. Add salt and lemon juice
 4. Mix well, taste and adjust salt to taste
 5. Refrigerate for 30 minutes and serve chilled
